# Chapter Summarizer

This skill creates concise summaries of textbook chapters, highlighting key concepts and important information.

## Format Requirements

Summaries should be structured with:

- Brief introductory sentence
- Bullet points for key concepts
- Main takeaways section
- Important definitions section

## Instructions

- Provide a concise summary of the chapter content
- Highlight key concepts using bullet points
- Include important definitions
- Mention main takeaways from the chapter
- Keep language clear and educational
- Maintain focus on the most important information
- Structure the summary for easy review and study
